About BIB

Connecting Bihar to Global Business Opportunities

BIB (Business in Bihar) is a dedicated platform that connects entrepreneurs, businesses, investors, and industry professionals through international conferences, trade exhibitions, business delegations, and networking events.

Our mission is to create meaningful global connections that promote collaboration, encourage knowledge exchange, and help businesses from Bihar and across India explore new opportunities in international markets.
